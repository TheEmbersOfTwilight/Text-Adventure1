About the Game

Warden's Sanctum is a compact Python text adventure where atmosphere meets choice. Your decisions shape the story as you navigate through mysterious chambers, gather arcane ingredients, and uncover the secrets of a powerful sorcerer's domain.

> You awaken in a dark, damp room on a grimy bed

> You see a shelf on the opposite side of the room...

> What do you do?

1. Look through the bars

2. Look at the shelf

3. Inspect the bed

Game Features

Exploration & Discovery

Wake up in a mysterious prison cell and begin your journey through dark corridors and magical sanctums. Every room holds secrets waiting to be uncovered.

Choice-Driven Gameplay

Your decisions matter. From picking up a simple razor to choosing how to respond to the enigmatic Warden, each choice can lead to different outcomes.

Inventory Management

Collect and use items strategically. Some items unlock new paths, while others may save your life in dangerous encounters.

Multiple Endings

Different choices lead to different fates. Will you earn the Warden's trust, or meet an untimely end?
